Our promise to you

We're committed to providing the best possible service to our Members.

Service-oriented

Our commitment to aiding our Members, team members, and communities in attaining their financial goals and objectives is simply who we are and why we're here, with a love for service at the foundation of our existence. Because our Members are owners in a financial cooperative, offering outstanding service is central to all we do.

We will build relationships.

We will establish a positive and professional relationship with you and lay the groundwork for our future collaboration. We feel that behaving in a friendly, open, and enthusiastic manner will enhance our relationship with you.

We will demonstrate trust and respect.

In every interaction, we will cherish your trust and show mutual respect. We'll pay attention to your needs and use all of our experience and resources to make sincere recommendations for improving your financial situation.

We will partner for security.

We will respect your privacy and do everything we can to keep your information secure in our systems, processes, and procedures, as well as educate you on how to protect your identity, privacy, and private information.

We will be ambassadors.

We will act as ambassadors at all times, because we know that our actions and interactions with you and within the communities we serve are a reflection of us. We will carry this through in everything we do.

We will show commitment.

Every day, we will devote our entire attention to servicing you. Your requests will be handled with a sense of urgency and a genuine gratitude for your time and business.

We will do the right thing.

We will make decisions based on honesty, ethics, and collaboration. We take responsibility for every interaction you have with our company and endeavor to offer you with the best possible service.
